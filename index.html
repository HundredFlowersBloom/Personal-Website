About [me]:


Hi, I'm Maite! I'm a first-year master's student at the University of Toronto, supervised by Prof. Ningning Xie. Previously, I did my undergrad at McGill University. My research interests are generally in type systems and functional programming. In my spare time, I enjoy watching movies, baking bread, and photography.

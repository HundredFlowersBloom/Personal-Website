Hi! My name is Maite, I'm a fourth-year Computer Science undergraduate at McGill University, where I have been working with Professor Brigitte Pientka. My research interests are in making formal verification simpler for real-world applications. Although software design has become easier and easier, we still lack good tools to be confident in the programs we use. Verification, particularly when tightly integrated into the tools that developers use (e.g. through novel programming languages and synthesizing correct-by-construction code), can help us attain more certainty in the systems that govern our day-to-day lives. In my spare time, I enjoy movies, sourdough baking, and photography.
